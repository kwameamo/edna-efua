The Happiest Memory

Fri, 04 Oct 2024


Thrilled about the future
Bagging the bucks
Securing tomorrow
Our blameless minds exuding the best of us
Nothing could stop us!


Vibrant, Vehement, promising and expectant!
Seeing the grass, green on the other side.
Plot twist; Quick turn to life
Still expectant but lost our liveliness
We could go hours on the phone,
Talking about how green the grass is,
Beaming in smiles, guffawing and painting our minds green


Today,
We lost our stir to life
Finding career paths,
The true essence to life,
Keeping the people we love and our innocence?
It’s a heavy duty!
We barely see each other
Hardly talk
Feels like we losing ourselves?
We’d rather be alone, figuring it out.


Seeing the grass, I soliloquy:
The grass is greener from far,
In their yard,
But oh!
The little row
They have to hoe
And that’s hard!
As time flashes so quickly
And as the leaves age, brown from green
You question:
Will we find ourselves when time has aged us;
Brawny to Feeble?
