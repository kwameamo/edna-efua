The Forbidden Tongue

Monday December 20, 2022


The forbidden tongue
It is a crime
It is an abomination
It is a taboo
To speak our own tongue


Once within the premises of the center of knowledge
Speak not your own tongue
But the white man’s
In the order of white men in black skins
who are our own Kinsmen


Our tongue goes by the name vernacular
Spear headed by the Opium of Oppression
Yes, a Foreign tongue is Literacy
But an African tongue is Illiteracy
Yes, Some human somewhere has the gut to
tell us to silence our tongues


And because of this, some of us have become
victims of shame
To the neglect of our own dialect
Our tongue is our life
That the enemies seek to destroy
It is our heritage and identity
Awaiting total elimination
